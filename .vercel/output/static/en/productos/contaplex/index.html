FAQ

ContaPlex questions

Is ContaPlex authorized by SUNAT?
Yes. ContaPlex complies with all SUNAT specifications for electronic books, SIRE, RCE and RVIE. It's on the official approved software list.
Can I migrate my data from another system?
Yes. Our team handles the migration: balances, historical records, chart of accounts and configuration. No extra cost on Business and Gold.
Does it include training for my team?
Always. Every implementation includes 3 live training sessions with your accountant and admin staff, plus unlimited access to DGD Academy online.
What if SUNAT issues new rules?
Codeplex updates the system automatically. You don't have to do anything — updates are deployed in the cloud without disrupting operations.
How do you protect my accounting data?
AWS servers in Peru/Brazil region, AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest, automatic backups every 6 hours with 90-day retention.
Do you run automatic backups?
Yes. Backups every 6 hours with 90-day retention. You can download your backups anytime from your panel.
How are updates billed?
Included. All ContaPlex updates, including regulatory changes and new features, are covered by your monthly plan.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, no fine print. Cancel with 30 days' notice and export all your data in standard formats (Excel, XML, PDF).

Ready to start with ContaPlex?

Implementation, migration and training included. We walk with you every step.
