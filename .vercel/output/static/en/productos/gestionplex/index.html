FAQ

GestiónPlex questions

Does it work for restaurants with multiple rooms?
Yes. GestiónPlex supports multiple rooms, table maps, kitchen dispatch per station and integrated delivery.
Can I use the POS offline?
The POS has offline sales mode. It syncs automatically when connection is restored.
How many users can I have?
All plans include unlimited users. You only pay for active warehouses.
Does it handle series and batches?
Yes, GestiónPlex handles series, batches, expiration dates and full traceability.
Does it integrate with my ContaPlex?
Native integration. Sales generate accounting entries automatically.
Does it work with barcodes?
Yes, USB, Bluetooth and mobile-app-integrated readers.
Do you handle migration from my current system?
Yes. Catalog, initial stock, customers and suppliers migrated at no cost on Premium and Gold.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, with 30 days' notice. We export all your data in Excel.
